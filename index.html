Turkish Airlines Miles&Smiles: Miles, Awards & Credit Cards

Miles&Smiles is the loyalty program of Turkish Airlines and a major player in the Star Alliance network. This guide explains how miles are earned, how redemptions and award zones work, elite tiers, and how credit cards and bank points boost your mileage growth.

Best Cards for Earning Miles&Smiles Miles

1. What the Miles&Smiles Program Is

Miles&Smiles is Turkish Airlines’ frequent-flyer program with strong Star Alliance connectivity. It is used both for long-haul premium travel and for affordable regional redemptions across Europe, the Middle East, Asia and North America.

  • Miles&Smiles miles – earned via flights, credit cards, transfers and partners.
  • Star Alliance coverage – wide route network with award access.
  • Multiple earning channels – including bank transfer partners.

2. How Miles&Smiles Miles Are Earned

You can earn Miles&Smiles miles from:

  • Turkish Airlines flights – miles and status miles are awarded based on cabin and fare.
  • Star Alliance partners – credit eligible flights to Miles&Smiles.
  • Co-branded credit cards in selected countries.
  • Bank point transfers – a key earning channel globally.
  • Hotels, rental cars and lifestyle partners that allow mileage credit.

Many members earn more from credit card spend and bank transfers than from flying, especially outside Turkey.

3. How Credit Cards Boost Miles&Smiles Earnings

Credit cards accelerate mileage growth significantly. Depending on the region, you may have:

  • Co-branded Miles&Smiles cards that earn miles directly.
  • Bank rewards cards with transfer capability to Miles&Smiles.
  • Welcome bonuses large enough for long-haul awards.
  • Transfer bonuses offered during promotions.

For many users, the best strategy is to collect flexible bank points and convert them into Miles&Smiles when ideal availability or transfer bonuses appear.

4. Redemption Options in Miles&Smiles

Miles&Smiles miles can be redeemed for:

  • Turkish Airlines flights in all cabins, including long-haul business class.
  • Star Alliance flights across global partner airlines.
  • Upgrades on eligible Turkish Airlines fares.
  • Star Alliance award zones with region-based pricing.
  • Other partner redemptions depending on market.

Many travelers use Miles&Smiles for long-haul business-class awards, where mileage pricing can be strong relative to cash fares.

5. Miles&Smiles Elite Status Tiers

The elite structure typically includes:

  • Classic – base tier.
  • Classic Plus – entry elite tier.
  • Elite – equivalent to Star Alliance Gold, with lounge access.
  • Elite Plus – top published tier with maximum benefits.

While credit cards don’t usually provide alliance-wide status, strong earning via cards helps you qualify faster by enabling more flying or upgrades.
